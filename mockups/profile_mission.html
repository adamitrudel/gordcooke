MISSION & VALUES

Gord’s mission is to help improve the quality, comfort, durability and energy efficiency of new homes in Ontario and North America through education and innovative products.

Goals

To provide technical representation of innovative energy efficient products to the building industry.
To provide products for the improvement of indoor air quality.
To provide services and support to builders to help improve building practices and meet and exceed building program guidelines.
To provide technical and sales & marketing training to the housing market and related building industries to lessen the impact of building on the environment.

Values

Gord VALUES :

  • The outdoor and indoor living environments. All of the products and services he provides are aimed at lessening the environmental footprint of building houses without compromising health or comfort.
  • His business relationships. Gord endeavours to build long term relationships with his clients, based on honesty, trust, good service and mutual respect.
  • His staff and associates. Gord strives to strengthen their individual understanding and knowledge base by encouraging professional development, and by providing strong leadership and mentoring to maintain the integrity of his company.
  • His reputation. Gord takes pride in being a leader in new building technologies, being easy to work with and for, and in being a first call resource for his clients.
